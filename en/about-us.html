Tradition meets digitalization

One team for every case.

Professional sport demands maximum focus - on performance, competition and development. Everything else needs to work smoothly in the background. Our team works like a well-rehearsed squad: each advisor is a specialist in their field and takes on exactly the tasks that matter for your situation. Coordination is handled centrally, so all decisions are aligned - efficiently, discreetly and with foresight. This avoids a patchwork of individual decisions and creates a clear strategy that supports you beyond your active career.

Our focus

We support professional athletes and performance-oriented people holistically, with a focus on the special requirements of their careers: fluctuating and time-limited income, international engagements and contracts, protection of performance capacity and wealth, structured wealth building for the time after sport.
Our aim is to structure financial decisions so you can fully focus on your sport - today and in the long term.
